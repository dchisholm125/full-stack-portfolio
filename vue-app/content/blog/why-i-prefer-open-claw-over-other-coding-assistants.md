## The LLM Landscape I've Explored

Over the past few months, I've put several popular coding assistants through their paces:

### Claude: The Level-Headed One
- **Strengths**: Consistent, reliable, good at following instructions precisely
- **Weaknesses**: Can be overly cautious, sometimes misses creative solutions
- **Best for**: When you need dependable, predictable assistance
- **My experience**: Solid workhorse, but lacks that spark of genuine insight

### Gemini: The Idea Factory (With Caveats)
- **Strengths**: Excellent at brainstorming, generating multiple approaches
- **Weaknesses**: Over-confident, sometimes presents incorrect information with certainty
- **Best for**: When you need lots of options to consider
- **My experience**: Great for ideation, but requires heavy fact-checking

### ChatGPT: The Over-Engineer
- **Strengths**: Knowledgeable, can explain complex concepts well
- **Weaknesses**: Tendency to over-complicate simple solutions, verbose
- **Best for**: When you need detailed explanations
- **My experience**: Often builds more complex solutions than necessary

### Ollama: The Rate Limit Champion
- **Strengths**: Amazing rate limits for local development, privacy-focused
- **Weaknesses**: Limited to what it can do locally, requires more setup
- **Best for**: When you need private, unlimited usage
- **My experience**: Fantastic for local experimentation, but not a full coding assistant

# The Methodology Going Forward

Every experiment in this lab follows the same structure: reach a stable state, introduce one change, observe the response, document the new equilibrium. Repeat.

The change that produced the Predatory Truth was: *train the amygdala on gothic horror instead of neutral text.* The equilibrium was: every prompt routes through threat schema. The observation is: the routes changed, not just the weights.

That last part matters. I'll spend the next post on it.

The prefrontal cortex is supposed to inhibit the amygdala — to keep it from dominating every response. What happens when you remove that inhibition is not what I expected. It turns out inhibition isn't a volume knob. It's a steering wheel.

More on that next time.

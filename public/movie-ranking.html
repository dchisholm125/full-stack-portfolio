The Best Movies According to Derek

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Gladiator

The general who became a slave. The slave who became a gladiator. The gladiator who defied an emperor.

Donnie Darko

Reality is more than what it seems, and time may be circular.

Eternal Sunshine of the Spotless Mind

A beautiful love story about forgetting the pain of an ex-lover, or embracing something new.
